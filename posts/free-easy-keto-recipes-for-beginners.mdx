## **My Favorite Easy Keto Breakfast Ideas**

Breakfast is my favorite part of keto because it keeps me full and energized.

### **Egg Muffin Cup Recipe**

This is one of my go to **free keto recipes**

**Ingredients**

* Eggs
* Spinach
* Cheese
* Salt
* Pepper

**Steps**

1. Whisk eggs
2. Add spinach and cheese
3. Pour into muffin cups
4. Bake until set

### **Simple Avocado Bowl**

This is a quick keto meal perfect for beginners.

**Ingredients**

* Avocado
* Olive oil
* Salt
* Pepper

I mash everything together and eat it with a side of boiled eggs.

### **No Bake Keto Yogurt Cup**

I mix sugar free yogurt with chia seeds and nuts for a **no bake keto** option that takes one minute.

I track my carb intake and watch for signs like steady energy and reduced hunger.

### **Can I do keto on a budget**

Yes. I use low cost ingredients like eggs chicken and spinach.

### **Do I need to count every carb**

In the beginning I counted carefully to learn my limits. It became easier with time.

Not required but helpful when I need quick fuel.
