<details>
  <summary>What kind of ingredients will I need for these easy keto meals?</summary>
  <p>The recipes primarily use common, readily available ingredients like eggs, meats, cheese, leafy greens, healthy fats (olive oil, butter, avocado oil), and low-carb vegetables (broccoli, cauliflower). You won't need expensive or hard-to-find items.</p>

<details>
  <summary>Do these recipes require special keto sweeteners or flours?</summary>
  <p>Many of the savory meals avoid sweeteners altogether. For baking or occasional treats, we focus on easily sourced **keto-friendly sweeteners** (like Erythritol or Stevia) and common low-carb flours (like **Almond Flour** or Coconut Flour).</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>What is the most important thing a keto beginner should track?</summary>
  <p>The most crucial thing to track is your **net carbohydrate intake**, aiming for typically 20–50 grams per day to achieve and maintain **ketosis**. Tracking protein and fat intake is also important, but carbs are the priority when starting.</p>
</details>
